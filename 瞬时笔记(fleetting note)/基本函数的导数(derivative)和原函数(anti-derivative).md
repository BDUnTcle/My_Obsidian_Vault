# Background & Materials
---
1. **幂函数(Power Function)**
	1.  $f(x)=x^n$
	2. $f'(x)=nx^{n-1}$
2. **指数函数(Exponential Function)*
	1. $f(x)=e^x$
	2. $f'(x)=e^x$
3. **对数函数(Logarithmic Function)**
	1. $f(x)=ln(x)$
	2. $f'(x)=\frac{1}{x}$
4. **三角函数(Trigonometric Functions)**
	1. $f(x)=sin(x),f'(x)=cos(x)$
	2. $f(x)=cos(x),f'(x)=-sin(x)$
	3. $f(x)=tan(x),f'(x)=sec^2(x)$
