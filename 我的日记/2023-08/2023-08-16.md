It's including 3 parts: the Notes area, CUE area and Summary area. ^5dSTwt2J

in the Notes area, students take notes about what they hear in the lecture.
Students do not need to write all words about what professor says. 
Just take down the important details, using symbles, pictures, abbreviation or whatever skills that can make student understand the content well.  ^isVyn9Uc

the cue zone, is designed to let student thinking about what they write down and write 
questions for themselves.
It can make student have a higher lever comprehension about the contents. ^QsUR4pUN

The summary area is to let students summarize the notes
It's need be done as soon as possible after class.
And it's very important, should not be skipped. ^BsDvciaV
